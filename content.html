Boost Online Revenue

With the online check-in system, you offer a more personalised, convenient and quicker way for your guests to check-in. Send an email to all your upcoming guests 3 days prior to their arrival date, inviting them to complete their check-in online. Engage with them prior to their arrival and enhance the guest experience. Guests can complete their check-in process using their own personal devices - laptop, tablet or mobile. It helps them to save time, bypass reception and avoid the usual check-in queues. The online check-in will help your hotel to comply with post-COVID-19 social distancing requirements.
