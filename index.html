The Glenroyal Hotel, perfectly positioned on Dublin’s doorstep. That means you’re close enough to the city to do everything you want to do yet far enough feel you’ve escaped.

We’re just a gentle stroll into the ancient university town of Maynooth, 20 minutes into Dublin and close to excellent train and bus services to connect you to the rest of Ireland. This is exactly where you want to be.

Come and Stay

This is your four-star home-from-home, famous for our warm welcome and relaxed elegance. The hotel you’ll want to come back to time and time again because it feels so right. With fantastic accommodation, excellent value and two of the best restaurants in Kildare, our stars are just four of the many reasons you’ll love the Glenroyal.

Stay with us

Eat and drink

This is where you’ll find two of Maynooth and Kildare’s favourite restaurants and bars. Pop in to the recently refurbished the Arkle Bar and Restaurant and savour that relaxed, stylish feel and atmosphere which makes it so popular amongst both locals and hotel guests. Or wander in to the Shoda Market Café which specialises in fresh, wholesome, healthy local food.
