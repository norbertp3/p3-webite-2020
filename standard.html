All-Inclusive Wedding Packages

The best weddings appear effortless. Our all-inclusive wedding packages offer exceptional value and include everything required for a perfect celebration: red carpet welcome with Champagne, 4-course meal with wine, centrepieces on each table: and all the little details like personalised menus and coloured bows on the chairs to match your theme. Celebrate your wedding with us at the Glenroyal Hotel with complete packages from €50.00 per person. We would be delighted to assist you with any additional items or arrangements you would like to include for your special day.
